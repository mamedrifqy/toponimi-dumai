Landscape Changes at the Riparian Area of Rawapening in 2016 and 2020

Data Visualization

Lake Rawapening is a unique landscape that naturally provides provisioning, regulatory, cultural, and supporting ecosystem services. On the other side, Rawapening Lake is an open access area that is subject to be vulnerable to any economic activities that lead to environmental degradation. The data itself extracted from 2016 High-Resolution Satellite Imagery of Research Area and 2020 Aerial Photo of Research Area collected from the Laboratory of Environmental Geomorphology and Disaster Mitigation, Faculty of Geography Universitas Gadjah Mada.
